The colors overlayed on the District Council Constituency Areas (DCCAs) are corresponding to the percentages of economically active households with income less $10,000. The color intensity is corresponding to the quartile values below.
■1st quartile: <6%
■2nd quartile: 7 - 11%
■3rd quartile: 12% - 17%
■4th quartile: > 18%


DCCAs with red border are those with above median level (>75%) of 25 to 64 years old residents whose highest educational attainment is secondary / sixth form education or below.
