The colors overlayed on the District Council Constituency Areas (DCCAs) are corresponding to the percentages of economically active households with income above $60,000. The color intensity is corresponding to the quartile values below.
■1st quartile: <3%
■2nd quartile: 3 - 8%
■3rd quartile: 9% - 20%
■4th quartile: > 20%


DCCAs with cyan border are those with above median level (>25%) of 25 to 64 years old residents whose highest educational attainment is post-secondary.

Click on the DCCAs for detail information.
